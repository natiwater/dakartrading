Ehiopian Coffee

Known as the birthplace of coffee, Ethiopia offers some of the finest coffee beans in the world. Our Ethiopian coffee is renowned for its unique flavor profile, characterized by a rich aroma, fruity and floral notes, and a smooth, full-bodied taste. Sourced from the highlands, where the climate and altitude create ideal growing conditions, our coffee beans are hand-picked and carefully processed to preserve their exceptional quality. Whether you prefer a light, medium, or dark roast, our coffee is sure to provide an unparalleled coffee experience.

Quality Meat

Premium Meat Products: Dakar Trading PLC specializes in high-quality meat products, including beef, lamb, and goat. Our meats are sourced from healthy, grass-fed animals, ensuring they are rich in flavor and nutrients. We adhere to stringent quality control standards to ensure our meat is tender, juicy, and free from antibiotics and hormones. Our meat products are perfect for a variety of culinary applications, from gourmet dishes to everyday meals, providing a reliable source of protein and essential nutrients.

Pure Oilseed Products

We offer a range of oilseeds, including sesame seeds, sunflower seeds, and flaxseeds. These seeds are known for their high oil content, nutritional value, and versatility. Our sesame seeds, for example, are prized for their nutty flavor and are used in a variety of culinary applications, from baking to cooking. Sunflower seeds are a great source of healthy fats, protein, and vitamins, making them a popular snack and ingredient in health-conscious diets. Flaxseeds are rich in omega-3 fatty acids, fiber, and antioxidants, offering numerous health benefits.

Nutritious Pulse supply

Our pulses include chickpeas, lentils, beans, and peas, which are all excellent sources of protein, fiber, vitamins, and minerals. These pulses are essential ingredients in many cuisines around the world and are valued for their ability to provide sustained energy and support a healthy diet. Our pulses are carefully selected and processed to ensure they are of the highest quality, making them an ideal choice for soups, stews, salads, and other dishes. They are also a great option for vegetarian and vegan diets, providing essential nutrients in a plant-based form.

Try our Spices

Our selection of spices is known for their vibrant flavors, aromatic qualities, and health benefits. These spices are carefully harvested and processed to retain their potency and freshness. Using our spices can elevate the flavor of any dish, adding depth, warmth, and a touch of the exotic. They are perfect for both home cooks and professional chefs looking to create authentic and flavorful meals. Whether you need a subtle hint of spice or a bold, robust flavor, our spices are versatile and can enhance any culinary creation
